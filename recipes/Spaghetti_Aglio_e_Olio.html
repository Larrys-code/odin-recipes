Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

It's pasta covered in oil and garlic.

Ingredients

  • some spaghetti
  • enough olive oil
  • as much sliced garlic as you can slice
  • red pepper flakes, or not I don't care
  • salt and pepper, I do care about this don't forget it
  • an amount of chopped fresh Italian parsley
  • a lot of cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti for 600 seconds
  2. At the same time, put the oil and garlic in a cold pan. Cook it a bit.
  3. Put the pepper and stuff on the pasta. Pour in the oil and garlic mixture.
  4. Serve with cheese.
